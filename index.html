WE GIVE YOU THE INVENTION WORKFLOW TOOLS TO SUCCEED

Patentory is a web platform that serves all stages of invention workflow through a smart, intuitive, and easy interface. For small technology companies, entrepreneurs, and inventors, we provide the tools to search and analyze patents, automatically prepare and file provisional patent applications, and find the right professionals who will help you the rest of the way

  • PATENT SEARCH

    Full text and fielded search options to identify patents and patent applications fast. Our database includes every US patent that ever issued and it is always up to date

  • PATENT ANALYSIS

    Industry's first analytic patent evaluation tool that puts the control in your hand. We compute and update nearly two dozen metrics that you can combine in evaluating patents

  • PATENT FILING

    Intelligently designed web interface to capture and organize your invention, and to automatically create a provisional patent application

  • PROFESSIONALS NETWORK

    Curated network of patent law firms, patent licensing and brokerage firms, and other professionals to help you protect your invention and benefit from it

OUR TARGET MARKET

For technology companies, our platform standardizes the process of capturing inventions and filing patents timely, and enables them to evaluate own patents and competitors

For entrepreneurs and individual inventors, we provide capable, reliable tools to evaluate and protect their inventions, and connect them with the right professionals

For patent professionals, our platform gives comprehensive patent search and review capabilities, as well as the ability to annotate patents, create and share reports

ABOUT PATENTORY

RAISON D'ETRE

Patents are an increasingly important asset class. In fact, they are the most valuable assets of many technology companies. Each year half a million US patent applications are filed, and the annual value of patent related transactions exceed $20B. However the tools needed to understand, search, evaluate patents, and file patent applications are too exclusive, too costly, and too esoteric for all but the specialized IP professionals and large companies. Patentory exists to transform the way we work with patents
